Welcome to Dream Wedding Studios! A powerful photo or video is the best and liveliest way to tell a story. We are a Wedding Photography Studio in Pune, India and are known for creating stories from the best day in your life. We started in 2015 and then have created hundreds of love stories. We are passionate, hard-working and motivated team; we love Maharashtrian weddings, and can’t wait to be a part of yours!
